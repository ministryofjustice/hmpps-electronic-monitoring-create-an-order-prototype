Electronic monitoring required HDC releases and re-releases

Device wearers must not be released or re-released on HDC on or after 2 September 2026.

Exception: A device wearer may be re-released only if all of the following apply:

  • They were on HDC in the community./li>
  • They were recalled during their HDC period on or after 2 September 2026.
  • The recall was under Section 255(1)(b) (inability to monitor).
  • They are being re-released.
  • They meet HDC policy, eligibility criteria, and pass the risk assessment.

If you are unsure about eligibility, check with the COM.
