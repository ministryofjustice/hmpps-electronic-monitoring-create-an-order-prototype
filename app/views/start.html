Apply, change or end an Electronic Monitoring Order (EMO)

Use this service to:

  • submit an EMO application form electronically to EMS replacing sending it by email
  • view and download your submitted application forms
  • submit a change to an EMO application form

If you don't have an account:

  • view the timetable to see if your team uses this service to submit applications. Your team may not be using the online service yet
  • if your team is using this service, follow the instructions to get a new account

This service does not yet allow you to:

  • request a visit for refits or equipment checks. For example, if the battery is not working or there is a medical reason for a refit
  • notify EMS of a change to the responsible officer
  • notify EMS of a change to the curfew hours or address for a device wearer serving a community sentence
  • request installation at a prison for a high-risk device wearer requiring GPS monitoring
  • submit a 'Special' EMO application for a Serious Organised Crime case, those convicted of Terrorist Offences, or assessed as Terrorist Connected and Terrorist Risk cases
How do I notify EMS of things that are not in the service?

How do I request a visit for refits or equipment checks or change the Responsible Officer?

Use the service request portal if you have access

If you don’t have access mail EMSEnforcement@ems.co.uk.

How do I notify EMS for a change in curfew hours or address for a device wearer serving a community sentence?

Complete the 'PCSC address change form' Word document and send it to LPAdmin@ems.co.uk.

How do I request installation at a prison or probation office?

  • all prisons can request installation at a probation office for Alcohol Monitoring using the service
  • prisons that are part of the “Install at Source” pilot can request installation at the prison for all monitoring types using the service
  • for GPS monitoring for a high-risk device wearer, the prison should submit a standard order, and probation must contact the EMS Liaison Lead for their region to request installation at the prison

How do I send a 'Special' order for a high-risk device wearer?

  • complete the EMS NSD CPPC SOC order form
  • send the completed form by email to emCCSpecialCase@ems.co.uk
